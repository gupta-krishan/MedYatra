❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM and any UPI app), all debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and net banking from SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB, BOB and all other major Indian banks.
What is the difference between Basic ($20) and Premium ($40)? +
Basic gives you a hospital shortlist, cost estimate, visa letter and email support. Premium adds a pre-travel surgeon video call, airport pickup, 24/7 WhatsApp concierge, interpreter services, accommodation help and 3-month follow-up. Most patients who are actually travelling choose Premium — it covers everything end-to-end.
How quickly will you respond after payment? +
Within 4 hours on any day of the week. After payment you'll receive a WhatsApp message from +91 9717019785. Share your medical reports with us there and we'll begin your hospital matching immediately.
Is there a refund if my trip is cancelled? +
If your trip is cancelled before we start work — full refund. If we have already begun shortlisting — 75% refund or full transfer to a future trip. Contact us at +91 9717019785 and we will always find a fair solution.
