❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the $200 plan actually cover? +
The $200 Premium Plan is a one-time payment per medical trip. It covers everything from hospital shortlisting, a pre-travel video consultation with a specialist, medical visa invitation letter, airport and hospital logistics, 24/7 WhatsApp concierge throughout your stay, interpreter services, accommodation help, and 3 months of post-treatment remote follow-up. There are no hidden charges or per-service fees.
Is the $200 in addition to my treatment cost? +
Yes. The $200 is MedYatra's coordination and concierge fee. Your hospital treatment cost is paid separately directly to the hospital. We negotiate the best rates on your behalf and ensure full transparency on all hospital bills before you commit.
How soon after paying will you contact me? +
We respond within 4 hours on any day of the week. You'll receive a detailed intake form to share your medical reports, and our care coordinator will call you within 24 hours to understand your case and begin the hospital matching process.
Do I need a medical visa to come to India for treatment? +
Yes, most international patients require an Indian Medical (e-Medvisa) visa. Our Premium Plan includes preparation of the hospital invitation letter required for this application. We guide you through the full visa process — it typically takes 3–5 business days to receive online approval.
What languages do your team speak? +
Our team and partner interpreters speak English, Arabic, French, Bengali, Swahili and Hindi. We can also arrange specialist interpreters for Dari, Pashto, Yoruba and other languages upon request at no extra charge.
Is there a refund if my trip is cancelled? +
If your medical trip is cancelled before we begin hospital shortlisting, we offer a full $200 refund. Once work has commenced, we can transfer the plan to a future trip or refund $150. We handle every case with empathy — please contact us and we will always find a fair solution.
